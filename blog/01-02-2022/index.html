Your Definitive Guide To Vaporwave in Canada

By Will Kopil

As a Canadian vaporwave artist I love to see the community that Canada fosters for the vaporwave scene. I enjoy listening to music from a wide variety of genres and artists. These are just some of the top vaporwave producers and labels in Canada. I personally own albums by these artists as well as many of the others who contribute to the Canadian vaporwave scene, please check out the full list of Canadian vaporwave artist's below:

Blank Banshee :
Bandcamp

From Saint John, New Brunswick, many of us are familiar with this artist. Blank Banshee is a project by Patrick Driscoll wherein he pioneered the vaportrap style. His use of eclectic synths mixed with firing hit-hats and erratic vocal sampling is hard to forget. His hit Teen pregnancy has been engraved into my subconscious ensemblage of vapory goodness ever since I first heard it.

 

Eco Virtual :
Bandcamp

There is some speculation that Eco Virtual is an alias of blank banshee or a close friend as evidenced by this post . The artist is Canadian, however anonymity surrounds them as it is interwoven with the history of vaporwave. Self-branded as "Atmospheric Research & Analysis Music" you could swear you've heard some of this stuff on the weather channel.

 

Waterfront Dining
Bandcamp

Waterfront dining keeps the traditional sound of vaporwave alive. Heavily sample based, their music resonates deeply with 80s synth, R&B, and funk. You can really hear the late night Torontonian cityscape throughout the music. If you love the classics, you have got to check out Waterfront Dining!

 

R23X
Bandcamp

AKA Marc Junker, from Vancouver, British Columbia, this artist produces video game music along with a mix of other styles, not limited to but including glitch and hip-hop. His mix of break beat style drums and pitched down vaporwave vocals will bamboozle you.

 

 bliss3three
Bandcamp

bliss3three takes a different approach to the genre, self identifying as vapornoise. They're music is scratchy, grainy, and often sounds like its coming out of a broken radio but you can still hear a coherent sound throughout. Sampling from 80s synth funk to upbeat videogame music you don't want to miss what this artist from Toronto, Ontario has to offer.

 

Graham Kartna
Bandcamp

Grahams music could be mistaken for the noise a defunct children's toy makes, if it was organized into a cohesive sound. Pitched up wacky vocals, detuned synths, and groovy beats can be found everywhere in his music. If you are looking for something that stays true to the vaporwave philosophy, but varies in its application be sure to check out this artist from Hamilton, Ontario. His works are midi masterpieces.

 

wosX (Wolfenstein OS X)
Bandcamp

wosX is credited with creating the earliest notable hardvapor release. On his album End of the world rave we are introduced to a brand new kind of vaporwave. It's a texture of hard hitting drums, and hi-fi synths, a real contrast to the classic smooth laid-back soundscape created in vaporwave. Explore some of the cutting edge sounds of vaporwave with this artist from Montreal, Quebec.

 

software systems hotline
Bandcamp

Software systems hotline's discography is short but sweet, another take on the classic sound. I've added them to make sure I bring you the most comprehensive list of artists possible.
